record_voice_over07 · Voice & Tone

How We Sound

DemandScience speaks in a clear, direct, human voice. We simplify B2B complexity without losing precision. We never use jargon when plain language works better.

"Empowering B2B Marketers to take back control of their growth."

Elevator Pitch

  • DemandScience helps B2B companies grow faster by connecting them with the right buyers at the right time. We provide demand generation solutions that are data-driven, scalable, and built for revenue impact — giving marketers verified buyer signals, invisible multi-channel execution, and continuous AI + human optimization.

Copy Rules

  • Headlines: Verb-led, outcome-first, ≤10 words
  • Body: Short sentences. One idea per paragraph.
  • CTAs: Action verbs — "Get", "See", "Start", "Request"
  • Jargon: Define on first use. Prefer plain language.
  • Emphasis: Bold only. Never italic for emphasis.
  • Numbers: Use numerals (3x, 247M+, 94%). Spell out "one" to "nine" in prose.

We Are

  • Clear and direct
  • Data-confident
  • Human and approachable
  • Ambitious
  • Expert without jargon
  • Outcome-focused

We Are Not

  • Vague or wordy
  • Data-obsessed to the point of cold
  • Casual or unprofessional
  • Arrogant
  • Acronym-heavy
  • Feature-focused
rule08 · Do & Don't

Brand Rules in Practice

When in doubt: left-align it, add more whitespace, and use less color. These rules exist because past violations damaged brand credibility.

check_circleDo

  • White backgrounds on all slides — no exceptions
  • Left-align all slide text — always
  • Poppins SemiBold (600) for all headings
  • Electric Blue (#0266F7) for CTA buttons and links
  • Google Material Symbols Rounded icons only
  • Add more whitespace — if it feels tight, add more
  • Authentic, candid photography (no posed portraits)
  • Chart colors: Navy → Blue → Medium Grey → Dark Grey
  • White chart backgrounds with minimal gridlines
  • Design approval before any external asset

cancelDon't

  • Gradient or navy slide backgrounds
  • Center body text — anywhere on slides
  • Montserrat, Lato, Open Sans, or any other font
  • CTA Red decoratively or as a background
  • Font Awesome, Heroicons, Feather, or Lucide
  • Multiple focal points per slide or screen
  • Stock photos with subjects looking at camera
  • Default Excel/Sheets colors in charts
  • Public assets without an approved template
  • Describe external requests as "quick" or "informal"

Need Brand Review?

External assets, new logo treatments, co-brands, and off-system designs must be reviewed before publishing. Contact the Design team.

gavel09 · Governance

Brand Enforcement System

Two-tier enforcement protects DemandScience's market presence. STOP conditions are absolute — no exceptions, no workarounds, no "just this once."

🚫 STOP — Halt All Work

  • Non-approved icon library
  • Font other than Poppins or Inter
  • Off-palette hex color
  • CTA Red used decoratively
  • Charts with default tool colors
  • Charts on colored backgrounds
  • New logo treatment or variant
  • Public asset without approved template
  • Co-brand or partner lockup
  • Ambiguous external-use request
  • "Quick external" request

⚠ Warning — Flag & Fix

  • Old hex values in use
  • Italic used for emphasis
  • Justified paragraph text
  • Heavy card shadow
  • Sharp-cornered cards
  • Navy/blue CTA button
  • Missing image alt text
  • Gradient on non-approved sections
  • Inconsistent card padding
  • Right-aligned body text

✅ No Review Needed

  • Internal presentations (approved template)
  • Client decks (approved template)
  • One-pagers (approved doc template)
  • Charts with approved color order
  • Material Symbols Rounded icons
  • Email using approved template
  • Web pages using component library
  • Internal Slack/comms graphics
  • Social posts using template
STOP Message Format — Issue Exactly As Written
