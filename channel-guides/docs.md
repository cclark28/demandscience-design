# Docs & PDFs Channel Guide

Reference `ds-brand-design.md` for all base rules. This file covers document and PDF-specific application.

---

## Page Setup

- **Margins:** 1" all sides
- **Background:** White throughout
- **Page size:** US Letter (8.5" × 11") standard; A4 for international
- **Columns:** Single column default; two-column allowed for feature sheets and comparison docs

---

## Typography (Point Sizes)

| Level   | Font    | Size    | Weight   | Color             |
|---------|---------|---------|----------|-------------------|
| Title   | Poppins | 28–32pt | Bold     | Navy `#05195F`    |
| H1      | Poppins | 22–24pt | Bold     | Navy `#05195F`    |
| H2      | Poppins | 18pt    | Bold     | Blue `#0066FC`    |
| H3      | Poppins | 14pt    | SemiBold | Navy `#05195F`    |
| Body    | Inter   | 11pt    | Regular  | Dark Grey `#1E293B` |
| Caption | Inter   | 9pt     | Regular  | Medium Grey `#64748B` |

- **Alignment:** Left throughout. No justified paragraphs.
- **Line spacing:** 1.3–1.5× for body text.
- **Paragraph spacing:** Add space after paragraphs rather than blank lines.

---

## Header & Footer

**Document header (first page):**
- DS logo — top left
- Document title — Poppins SemiBold, large
- Subtitle / date — Inter, medium grey

**Running header (subsequent pages):**
- Document title — small, medium grey, left
- Optional: section name, right

**Footer:**
- Page number — right-aligned, Inter 9pt, medium grey
- "Confidential" or "© DemandScience [year]" — left-aligned

---

## Callouts & Sidebars

- Background: Light grey `#F8F9FB` or Blue Light `#E8F2FF`
- Border radius: ~6pt
- Left accent bar: Navy or Blue, 3pt wide
- Use for: key stats, definitions, pro tips, important notes

---

## Tables

- Header row: Navy background, white bold text
- Body rows: alternate white / Blue Light `#E8F2FF`
- Totals row: Navy background, white bold text
- Borders: Light grey `#E5E7EB`, minimal

---

## CTA Elements

- Pink pill button (`#D52C5A`) when there's a clear reader action (download, request demo, contact)
- Link text: Blue `#0066FC`, underlined
- Place CTAs at: end of document, end of major sections, or dedicated CTA page
