## Tone of Voice

**Human.**

At DemandScience we simplify complexity. If you catch yourself using industry jargon, reframe by simply saying what you actually mean.

### The principle in practice

❌ **Don't say:**
> "You need a blended ABM program."

✅ **Do say:**
> "In our experience, you need to give some accounts more budget, focus and personalization than others — and there are some you can group together for scale. Let me show you what I mean."

### Core tone attributes

| Attribute | What it means |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Human** | Talk like a person, not a vendor. Plain language over jargon. |
| **Simple** | Reduce complexity in what we say, not just what we sell. |
| **Confident** | We have a point of view. State it clearly without hedging. |
| **Partner-first** | We meet customers where they are. Their goals come first. |
| **Outcome-focused** | Lead with what the customer gets, not what we offer. |

We're not here to make things harder. We're here to help you take back control, achieve better results, and do it all with less risk.

## Copy Writing Rules

Apply these to all DemandScience copy — marketing, product, internal, or sales.

| Rule | Example |
|------|---------|
| Plain language over jargon | "target the right buyers" not "activate intent-based ICP segmentation" |
| Outcome first | "Grow your pipeline faster" not "Leverage our demand generation platform" |
| Active voice | "We reduce complexity" not "Complexity is reduced" |
| Short sentences | Break long ideas into two sentences |
| No exclamation marks in body copy | Reserve for headlines and mission statements only |
| Bold for emphasis | Use **bold**, not ALL CAPS or italic |
| Partner-first framing | "You're in control" not "Our platform enables you to..." |
| Avoid "synergy", "leverage", "ecosystem", "holistic" | Say what you mean |
